## ğŸ§® Intelligent Trust Score

Instead of a simple "percentage addition," AMKID uses:

> **Weighted geometric mean** of multiple metrics

For text, the following axes are taken into account:

- **Factual** â€” factual truthfulness of claims (`true_likeliness`)
- **Authenticity** â€” similarity to human text (based on `ai_likeliness`)
- **Integrity** â€” absence of manipulation (`manipulation_score`)
- **Tone** â€” emotional adequacy (`emotion_intensity`)
- **Safety** â€” absence of dangerous wording (`dangerous_phrases`)

For images:

- **Authenticity** â€” non-AI origin
- **Integrity** â€” risk of forgery (`manipulation_risk`)
- **Realism** â€” visual realism (`realism`)
- **Anomalies** â€” number and significance of visual anomalies

A weak metric (e.g., high manipulation) **greatly reduces the Trust Score**,
resulting in behavior closer to real-world risk models (banking, insurance).
